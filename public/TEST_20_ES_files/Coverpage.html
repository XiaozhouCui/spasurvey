Dear staff,

Ensuring that BPA is a great place to work is important to me, as I imagine it is to all of us in this organisation. Accordingly, I am pleased to be undertaking this employee engagement survey.

Each one of you helps to shape and create the culture of our organisation. Your responses are important to help us measure the success of our actions and inform future strategies to do things even better. For these reasons, you are best placed to tell us what we’re good at and where we could improve, to work together, differently and for the future.

The Senior Leadership Team is absolutely committed to listening to what you have to say through this survey and taking action.

The survey is conducted by the independent research company Best Practice Australia (BPA) and runs from Monday, 11 November to Monday, 25 November 2019. All responses will remain confidential, and your answers cannot be traced back to you.

If you have any questions about the survey, please refer to our FAQs.

Thank you in advance for your willingness to participate. I am very much looking forward to receiving your feedback, and sharing the results with you in the new year.

Yours Sincerely,

Carol Jeffs
Chief Executive Officer
BPA
